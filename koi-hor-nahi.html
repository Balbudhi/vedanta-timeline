A Punjabi devotional song

Koi Hor Nahi Hai Mera

I have no one else but You

Devotional Punjabi addressed to the Divine Mother — specifically the Mother as bearer of the sacred flame (jyotī̃-wālī mātā), in the Vaiṣṇo Devī / Jwālā Jī / Śākta register. The song's core gesture is physical: a child clutching the hem of the Mother's garment and refusing to let go.

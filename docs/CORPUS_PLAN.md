# CORPUS_PLAN

Exhaustive list of Vedāntic thinkers (and one block of Trika comparators) to include in the interactive timeline. Each entry includes provenance-honesty about dating: most pre-Śaṅkara figures are reconstructed only via citation in later authors. Better to include and later cull than to omit.

Legend for `dates_tier`:

- `confirmed-from-records` — inscriptional, royal-court, or sectarian-chronicle attestation contemporary or near-contemporary
- `consensus-textual` — academic consensus from internal references, citations by datable authors, manuscript chronology
- `contested` — multiple defensible date-ranges, no scholarly consensus
- `oral-tradition-only` — dates known only from later sectarian hagiography or by inference from citation-evidence in much later authors

Legend for `ascription_tier`:

- `securely-authored` — undisputed in modern scholarship
- `traditionally-ascribed` — long-standing tradition assigns it; modern scholars accept majority but flag interpolation
- `school-ascribed` — sect or living tradition embraces it as the master's; academic opinion divided
- `disputed` — significant scholarly dispute over authorship
